Python Invoice Generator

Origin:

Making invoices by hand at my job took forever when there are 50-100 people that needed invoices. So I decided to make it easier to create invoices.

Purpose:

Make life easier at work, by looping through csv file of names that need invoices and printing the names on template invoices and incrementing the invoice number.

Project Length:

2 weeks on and off

Languages/Software Used:

Python, PyCharm
